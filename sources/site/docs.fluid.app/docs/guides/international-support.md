- **Umsatzsteuer-ID (USt-IdNr.)** - VAT identification number for businesses
- **Registration with Finanzamt** - Local tax office registration
- **Advance VAT returns** - Monthly or quarterly depending on revenue
- **Annual tax returns** filing requirement
- **Accounting records** must be maintained for at least 10 years
- **Handelsregister** - Commercial register entry for certain business types

### Turkey 🇹🇷

#### Payment Processing & Currency

**Primary Currency:** TRY (Turkish Lira ₺)

**Payment Methods Supported:**

- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- **Local Bank Transfers**
- **Digital Wallets** (supporting Turkish market)

**Payment Gateway:** Citcon

#### Address & Shipping Support

**Address Format:**

- Address Line 1 (required)
- Address Line 2 - Neighborhood/District (Mahalle) (optional)
- City/Municipality (İl) (required)
- Province (required) - All Turkish provinces populated in dropdown
- Postal Code (Posta Kodu) (5 digits, required)

#### Tax Compliance

**Tax Calculation Options:**

- **Fluid's Tax Solution** - Integrated tax calculation and compliance platform
- **Third-party integrations** - Support for external tax service providers

**Tax Structure:**

- **KDV (Katma Değer Vergisi):** 20% standard rate (Value Added Tax)
- **Reduced KDV rates:** 10% (basic food items), 1% (essential products)
- **Gelir Vergisi:** Progressive income tax on earnings
- **Tax reporting:** Required for all commercial transactions
- **GIB (Gelir İdaresi Başkanlığı)** - Revenue Administration compliance required

**Business Requirements:**

- **Vergi Kimlik Numarası (VKN)** - 10-digit Tax Identification Number for businesses
- **Registration with local Tax Office** - Tax office registration mandatory
- **Monthly VAT returns** for registered businesses
- **Annual tax returns** filing requirement
- **E-fatura (Electronic Invoice)** system for B2B transactions
- **Accounting records** must be maintained for at least 5 years

#### Language Support

**Checkout Experience:** Turkish, English **Mobile App:** Turkish, English

#### Enrollment Requirements

**Brand Partner Enrollment Form Fields:**

- **Date of Birth** - Must be 18+ years old (required)
- **Identity Verification:**
 - **T.C. Kimlik Numarası (Turkish National ID)** - 11-digit Turkish citizen identification number (required and validated)
- **Banking Information:**
 - **IBAN (International Bank Account Number)** - Turkish bank account for commission payouts (format: TR + 24 digits, required and validated)
- **Legal Compliance:**
 - **Law No. 6502** - Consumer Protection Act compliance
 - **14-day right of withdrawal** (Cayma Hakkı) for consumers under Distance Contracts Regulation
 - **30-day right of withdrawal** for direct sellers under Regulation on Direct Selling
 - **KVKK (Kişisel Verilerin Korunması Kanunu - Law No. 6698)** - Turkish Data Protection Law compliance (equivalent to GDPR)
 - **Regulation on Direct Selling** - Comprehensive direct selling compliance
 - **No mandatory purchase requirements** - Mandated by Turkish Direct Selling Regulation
 - **E-Ticaret Kanunu (Law No. 6563)** - E-Commerce Law compliance
 - **Tüketici Hakem Heyetleri** - Consumer Arbitration Committee dispute resolution
- **Income Disclosure:**
 - **Transparent earnings disclosure** required - Most direct sellers earn little to no income
 - **Anti-pyramid scheme provisions** - Product-based business model required by law
 - **No income guarantees** - Success depends on individual effort and market conditions

#### Legal Agreements

**Checkout Agreements (All Required):**

- **Preliminary Information Form (Ön Bilgilendirme Formu)** - Mandatory under Regulation on Distance Contracts
- **Distance Sales Agreement (Mesafeli Satış Sözleşmesi)** - Required by Law No. 6502
- **Privacy Policy (Gizlilik Politikası)** - KVKK compliance (Law No. 6698)
- **Return and Refund Policy (İade ve Geri Ödeme Politikası)** - 14-day consumer withdrawal right
- **Auto-Ship/Subscription Terms (Otomatik Gönderim Koşulları)** - Recurring billing disclosures

**Enrollment Agreements:**

- **Direct Seller Terms & Conditions (Doğrudan Satıcı Hüküm ve Koşulları)** - Comprehensive agreement including:
 - Independent contractor relationship
 - 30-day withdrawal right for direct sellers
 - No mandatory purchase requirements to join or remain as seller
 - Income disclosure statement with realistic earnings data
 - Anti-pyramid scheme provisions
 - KVKK data protection compliance
 - Product-based business model (not recruitment-based)
