Fluid is the we-commerce platform built for direct sales and multi-level marketing organizations. It brings together the tools your developers, merchants, and sales reps need—APIs for headless commerce, a Liquid-based theme engine, affiliate attribution, global payment processing, and a marketplace of plug-in apps (Droplets)—all in one place.

## What's in the Platform

Fluid is made up of several integrated components. Here's a quick map of what each one does.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Fluid Connect" icon="link">
    Bi-directional sync with your back-office in minutes. Supports Exigo, ByDesign, Pillars, InfoTrax, and Shopify.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Fluid Payments" icon="credit-card">
    Accept 300+ payment methods in 100+ countries with PCI-compliant vault storage.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Fair Share" icon="handshake">
    Multi-domain affiliate attribution via cookies, fingerprints, routes, and more.
  </Card>
  <Card title="We-Commerce Checkout" icon="bag-shopping">
    One-page checkout handling address validation, shipping, tax, inventory, and payments.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Fluid Login" icon="lock">
    Unified auth with SSO, MFA, magic links, social providers, and RBAC.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Droplet Marketplace" icon="store">
    Install premium e-commerce apps instantly with zero integration required.
  </Card>
  <Card title="App Builder" icon="mobile">
    Drag-and-drop builder that deploys to iOS, Android, Windows, and macOS.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Website Editor" icon="globe">
    Liquid-powered theme editor with built-in Fair Share and internationalization.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
